## Key points for model

- Chikungunya has VERY high arthralgia/myalgia (98.8%) — strongest discriminating symptom
- Fever present in only 68.7% at presentation (may reflect delayed presentation)
- Rash in 35.5%
- Regional acquisition follows outbreak dynamics — supports the live-outbreak prior layer
- Caribbean and SE Asia dominate, but shifts year to year with outbreaks
- Acquired in ~100 destinations globally
- Incubation: 1-12 days (from CDC Yellow Book, not this paper)
- Exposure route: mosquito (Aedes aegypti/albopictus)

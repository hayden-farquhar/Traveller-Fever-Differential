## Key points for model

- **Dengue dominates in SE Asia** (50.4%) — Thailand and Indonesia alone = 33.4%
- Fever in 87% (higher than chikungunya's 68.7% — useful discriminator)
- Myalgia 36.1% (vs chikungunya's 98.8% arthralgia — key differential)
- Rash in 28.9% (similar to chikungunya)
- Abdominal pain in 9.4% uncomplicated but 32.7% complicated — warning sign
- Headache 37.2% (not very discriminating)
- Incubation: 4-14 days (from CDC, not this paper)
- Exposure: mosquito (Aedes aegypti/albopictus)
